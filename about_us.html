About

Our vision is to create an engaging platform to promote women owned businesses. 2020 has been a difficult year for everyone and full of economic uncertainty but it has been amazing to see how many businesses have been created as a result of lockdown. Women are known to have less of a chance when it comes to receiving promotions in the workplace in comparison to their male peers and experience a significant disparity in the gender pay gap. With that said, there has been a massive boom in terms of virtual businesses with a focus towards homemade products. We aim to use this space to market all these incredible female led businesses with input from you to help keep expanding our featured businesses!
