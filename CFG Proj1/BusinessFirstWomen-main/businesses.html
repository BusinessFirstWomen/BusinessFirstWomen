Blaqbase

Blaqbase was founded by Benedicta Banga in 2019 as a shopping and discovery app. The app makes it easy to discover and support black owned businesses and find high quality and premium brands. Some of the brands available include beauty products such as Elenge, Flora and Curl and Hay London, and clothing and accessories including We Are Kin and Relic. The app aggregates multiple brands, which you can buy in one place using the single checkout - plus most of them deliver worldwide!
