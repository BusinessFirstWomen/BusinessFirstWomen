About Us

Our Mission

Our vision is to create an engaging platform to promote women owned businesses. 2020 has been a difficult year for everyone and full of economic uncertainty but it has been amazing to see how many businesses have been created as a result of lockdown. Women are known to have less of a chance when it comes to receiving promotions in the workplace in comparison to their male peers and experience a significant disparity in the gender pay gap. With that said, there has been a massive boom in terms of virtual businesses with a focus towards homemade products. We aim to use this space to market all these incredible female led businesses with input from you to help keep expanding our featured businesses!

Akili's Art

Akili is a Freelance Illustrator and Graphic Designer based in London. Akili creates unique prints and t-shirts using bright colours and eye catching designs. All prints and t-shirts are sustainably produced and packaged. The prints are produced by a carbon neutral company and are delivered using recyclable envelopes, 100% recycled paper tape and biodegradable bubble wrap. String Theory London

String Theory London are a home-decor company that creates naturally hand-dyed Macrame homewares, made with 100% cotton cords. One of their core values are sustainability hence all the products are mind whilst keeping the environment in mind. Every cord has been hand-dyed in their kitchen using only natural ingredients. Blaqbase

Blaqbase was founded by Benedicta Banga in 2019 as a shopping and discovery app. The app makes it easy to discover and support black owned businesses and find high quality and premium brands. Some of the brands available include beauty products such as Elenge, Flora and Curl and Hay London, and clothing and accessories including We Are Kin and Relic. The app aggregates multiple brands, which you can buy in one place using the single checkout - plus most of them deliver worldwide!

Box42

Box42 is a business founded with purpose by Caroline Haegman. In these changing times, Box42 aims to bring love, laughter and fun back into the home. Box42 can be shipped to anywhere in the UK and contains a full date night guide, fun activities, accessories, snacks, drinks and a curated playlist!
